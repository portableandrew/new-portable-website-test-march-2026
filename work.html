Amica — AI that helps separating couples move forward.

When couples separate, navigating asset division without lawyers is genuinely hard. Amica uses machine learning trained on a synthetic dataset co-created with family lawyers across every Australian state and territory — removing bias, removing cost, and giving people a fair path forward.

One of Australia's longest-running government AI deployments. Built in partnership with the Attorney-General's Department. Winner of the National Innovation Award 2020. Still live. Still delivering.

Sweep — Ending wage theft with AI.

Wage theft costs Australian workers billions each year. Sweep connects payroll and Modern Award data to automatically flag potential discrepancies before they become legal problems — giving employers confidence and protecting workers' rights.

Built in partnership with Fair Work specialists and funded through a $1.1M Business Research and Innovation Initiative (BRII) grant from the Australian Federal Government. Scaling nationally across multiple industries.

Hear Me Out — AI guided support for young Australians.

For young people experiencing trauma, accessing mental health support is too often a barrier in itself. Hear Me Out uses trauma-informed design and natural language processing to connect people with the right support — on their terms, in their language.

Built with accessibility and equity at the centre. Designed for young people where standard mental health services fail most. The approach puts safety, consent and human dignity before everything else.
