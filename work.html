Amica — AI that helps separating couples move forward.

When couples separate, navigating asset division without lawyers is genuinely hard. Amica uses machine learning trained on a synthetic dataset co-created with family lawyers across every Australian state and territory — removing bias, removing cost, and giving people a fair path forward.

One of Australia's longest-running government AI deployments. Built in partnership with the Attorney-General's Department. Winner of the National Innovation Award 2020. Still live. Still delivering.

Fair Work · AI Compliance

Sweep

Wage theft costs Australian workers billions each year. Sweep connects payroll and Modern Award data to automatically flag potential discrepancies before they become legal problems — giving employers confidence and protecting workers' rights. Built in partnership with Fair Work specialists and funded through a $1.1M BRII grant from the Australian Federal Government.

Hear Me Out

For young people experiencing trauma, accessing mental health support is too often a barrier in itself. Hear Me Out uses trauma-informed design and natural language processing to connect people with the right support — on their terms, in their language. Built with accessibility and equity at the centre.
