Our Journey

2004

Founded with Purpose

Portable was established with a singular mission: to use technology to create meaningful change for government and purpose-driven organisations.

2008

Expanding Impact

Expanded into product delivery and capability building, recognising that sustainable change requires more than just technology.

2015

Introducing Uplift Programs

Launched comprehensive training and capability uplift programs to empower organisations to build their own digital capacity.

2020

Leading AI Responsibly

Established AI research and strategy practice, focusing on responsible AI implementation in government and social sectors.

2024

Scaling GenAI Solutions

Launched AI with Portable — a comprehensive GenAI capability program helping organisations unlock the potential of artificial intelligence ethically.
