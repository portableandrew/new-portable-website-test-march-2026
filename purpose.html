Our Values

01
We care about doing good work
Quality work that matters. Pride in delivery. Not ticking the box. Collective respect for the plan and for each other's contributions.
02
We try new things
Curiosity, creativity, and purposeful experimentation. Safe to fail. We celebrate the learning that comes from exploration and thoughtful risk-taking.
03
We are in it together
Team-first mindset. No hero efforts. Commitment to one another. Honest relationships. Fun alongside work. We succeed because of each other.
04
We learn through feedback
Celebrate success. Reflect on failure. Give and receive respectful feedback. Build on strengths. We grow through honest, caring dialogue.
