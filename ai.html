Our Approach

Responsible AI isn't a constraint. It's the work.

We believe the highest-leverage AI work happens where responsible design, ethical governance and genuine community benefit intersect. That's not a limitation on what we can build — it's what makes what we build last.

01

Human outcomes before technology outcomes

We start with the people the system will affect — before we write a line of code. Every design decision traces back to a real human need, a community risk, or a question of equity.

02

Transparency by design

AI decisions that affect people's lives must be explainable. We build systems where the reasoning is auditable, the limitations are visible, and communities can hold the technology to account.

03

Equity-centred

Technology amplifies what's already there — including bias. We actively design to reduce disparity, particularly for communities historically underserved by government systems.

04

Production-grade, not prototype

A demo is not a product. We build AI that operates in complex, regulated environments at national scale — with the robustness, security and reliability that context demands.

05

Capability transfer, not dependency

Our goal is to make ourselves less necessary over time. We embed skills, build internal champions and design operating models that let your team own and evolve the AI long after we're gone.

06

All onshore, all in-house

ML, GenAI, NLP, data science, policy and training — all in-house, all onshore. The team you meet is the team that builds. No outsourced quality risk, no black box.

AI In Action

Machine Learning / Justice / Federal Gov

Amica

AI-powered divorce and asset division platform. Trained on a synthetic dataset co-created with family lawyers across every Australian state and territory. One of Australia's longest-running government AI deployments. 7+ years live, $35M in measured community benefit.

AI Compliance / Fair Work / Federal Gov

Sweep

Wage compliance AI that flags discrepancies between payroll and Modern Award data before they become legal problems. $1.1M BRII grant. Scaling nationally across multiple industries.

Mental Health / NLP / Youth

Hear Me Out

Trauma-informed AI guided support for young Australians. Combines NLP with equity-centred design to connect people with mental health resources at the moments they need it most.

GenAI / Strategy / CSIRO

AI Navigator

AI capability and wayfinding tool built with CSIRO. Helps government agencies understand their AI readiness, identify adoption pathways and build internal confidence in responsible AI implementation.
