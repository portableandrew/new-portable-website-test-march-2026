A state-wide strategy to improve the wellbeing of all Victorians. We led deep community research, systems mapping and co-design with government, community organisations and lived experience voices to shape policy that reflects real human needs.

Statewide community engagement spanning diverse populations and geographies

Department of Education

Understanding Youth Anxiety in Schools

A research and design project exploring the lived experience of anxiety among young people in Victorian schools. We partnered with students, teachers and mental health practitioners to understand systemic gaps and design more effective school-based responses.

Deep ethnographic research with students experiencing anxiety
Co-design of support pathways with school staff and clinicians
Service blueprint for school-based mental health response
Recommendations adopted into departmental policy

Orygen — National Youth Mental Health

ChatSafe — Safe Online Communication Guidelines

Designed in partnership with Orygen, Australia's leading youth mental health research centre, ChatSafe provides evidence-based guidelines for young people communicating about suicide and self-harm online. A landmark project at the intersection of digital behaviour change and suicide prevention.

Co-designed with young people with lived experience
Behaviour change framework tested across social platforms
Adopted by platforms and organisations internationally
Evidence base published in peer-reviewed research

How we work

Embedded, co-designed, evidence-led.

We embed researchers and designers into your organisation, work alongside your teams, and design with — not for — the communities you serve. Every engagement builds your internal capacity to sustain the work.

Research first

We don't start with solutions. We start with people. Deep qualitative research, lived experience engagement and systems mapping before a single concept is sketched.

Co-design throughout

Community members and practitioners aren't consulted — they're co-designers. Every stage involves the people whose lives are shaped by the outcome.

Test and iterate

We prototype early and test in real contexts. Concepts are stress-tested with diverse users before recommendations reach decision-makers.

Build capability

Every engagement grows your team's research and design capability. We run workshops, coach practitioners and leave you stronger than we found you.

Why Portable Design

Research you can trust

We recruit and engage with communities in genuine, respectful ways. Our research is rigorous, ethical and grounded in lived experience — not just stakeholder interviews.

Design for complexity

Government and purpose sector problems don't have simple solutions. We're experienced working in politically sensitive, high-stakes environments where getting it wrong matters.

Translating insight to action

Research that sits on a shelf has no value. We design for implementation from the start — with clear recommendations, prototypes and capability to take the work forward.

Whole-of-Portable integration

Design and technology aren't separate disciplines for us. Our designers work alongside engineers, delivery leads and AI specialists to build things that are both desirable and deliverable.
